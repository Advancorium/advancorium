Account Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your account and associated data for any app published by Advancorium, please contact us at:

advancoriumsl@gmail.com

To help us process your request, please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • The name of the app
  • A clear statement that you want your account deleted

What will be deleted

When your request is processed, we will delete your account information and associated app data, such as saved preferences and user-related records, unless retention is required for legitimate legal, security, fraud-prevention, or financial compliance reasons.

What may be retained

Certain limited information, such as billing, transaction, or legal compliance records, may be retained for the period required by applicable law or for fraud-prevention and security purposes.

Processing time

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days.
